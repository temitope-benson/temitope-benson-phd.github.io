Network Model

Currently in Progress and will be updated as more of the model is built

The mechanisms and dynamics that centrosome use to mature are unknown in any species. These dynamics are critically important to how the centrosomes nucleate microtubules which are required for the proper placement of the centrosomes and the division planes of cells. Asymmetric divisions of cells are required for stem cell maintenance in many organisms. Perturbations to division plane placement is a hallmark of cancer metastasis.

How cells properly position their centrosomes is unknown.

As part of my PhD thesis, I performed a literature review of the centrosome maturation process. I used the information from the literature review to build an interaction network of the centrosome maturation process. The network will be used in the future to build a mathematical model with Julia.

What Dynamics are required for the proper maturation of centrosome factors?

With this model we aim to investigate the dynamics and mechanisms required for proper maturation in order to gain a better understanding of the entire process of centrosome movement and placement.
